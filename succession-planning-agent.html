Role Expectations & Success Criteria
Organizations can define
Quantitative expectations (outcomes, responsibilities)
Qualitative and behavioral traits (leadership, judgment, influence)
KPIs and traits serve as reference anchors, ensuring successor evaluation is aligned to real role demands not generic leadership assumptions.

Identifying and Assessing Successors
Multiple potential successors can be enrolled for a role supporting inclusive, forward-looking succession pools rather than single-name nominations.
AI-Supported Successor Interviews
AI conducts structured, role-aligned interviews with each candidate to assess:
Capability and experience
Behavioral alignment
Decision-making and role readiness
These interviews are consistent, explainable, and auditable-while final decisions remain human-led.
Detailed Readiness Intelligence
For each candidate, the platform generates a comprehensive readiness profile:
Best-fit and near-fit identification
Transparent scoring with clear rationale
Actionable guidance on what near-fit candidates need to develop to become ready
This ensures succession planning leads directly to preparation, not just selection.
